Secret Santa SMS Service

Official Compliance & Disclosure Page

Service Description (Campaign Use Case)

This is a personal, automated text messaging service used exclusively for our annual Secret Santa gift exchange. Users text in a specific keyword (like 'SANTA') to opt-in, and will receive a single, automated text message containing the name of their assigned gift recipient.

This service is designed for low-volume, personal use only.

Required SMS Disclosures

Opt-In Consent: By texting our designated number with the sign-up keyword, you consent to receive up to two automated messages regarding the Secret Santa drawing.

Rates Disclosure: Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out Instructions: Reply **STOP** at any time to opt-out of all future messages. Reply **HELP** for assistance.

Privacy Policy Summary

We respect your privacy. We **do not share or sell** your phone number or any personal data collected through this SMS service with any third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. Your phone number is used solely to facilitate the Secret Santa drawing and notification.
